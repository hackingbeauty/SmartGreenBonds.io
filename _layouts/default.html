ABOUT SMART GREEN BONDS
Smart Green Bonds are DeFi financial instruments whose interest rates are based on the health of the environment. For example, less pollution yields better interest rates. Get paid in crypto to plant trees. Economic incentives powered by Smart Contract to incentivize regeneration.
